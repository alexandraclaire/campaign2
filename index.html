Body Image: What's going on?

The Facts

80% of 10 year old children are afraid of being "fat", and have dieted.

80%

70% of girls feel like they don't measure up to their peers.

70%

12% of teen boys are using steroids or supplements.

12%

More than 90% of girls aged 15-17 wish to change their appearance.

90%
The Effects

Obesity has risen by 300% in the last 30 years.
Meanwhile, other eating disorders are prevalent, with 13% of teenage girls having been affected by an eating disorder.

Who Can Help Me?

If you know someone affected by an eating disorder:

EDANZ
Eating Disorder Association New Zealand (EDANZ) was established by parents of people with eating disorders in New Zealand. They offer support and some advice for parents and carers who may be caring for someone with an eating disorder or would like help and support.

Phone: (09) 522-2679
Email: info@ed.org.nz
Web: www.ed.org.nz
